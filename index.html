Le Duy Dung is currently a research scientist in School of Information Systems, Singapore Management University. He earned his PhD in Data Science and Engineering from the same university, working with Associate Professor Hady W. Lauw. In his candidature, he has been recognized with SMU Presidential Doctoral Fellowship Awards and SMU PhD Student Life Awards. Formerly, he earned his Degree of Engineer in Mathematics and Informatics from Hanoi University of Science and Technology, Vietnam in 2014. His research interests are in recommender systems, information retrieval, and visual analytics, with publications in major data mining and AI venues such as AAAI, IJCAI, CIKM, and SDM.
