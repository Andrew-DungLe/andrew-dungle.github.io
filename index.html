Le Duy Dung is an Assistant Professor at College of Engineering and Computer Science, VinUniversity, where he leads the R2Studio reseach group on AI-empowered Search and Recommendation and IndoorCare research group on AI and Simulation for Smart Indoor Spaces. Previously, he was a senior data scientist in Ads and Personalization team, Grab Holdings Inc. and a research scientist in School of Information Systems, Singapore Management University (SMU). He earned his PhD in Data Science and Engineering from SMU, under the supervision of Associate Professor Hady W. Lauw. More details can be found in his CV (updated Jan 2024)
